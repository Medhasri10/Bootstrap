An e-commerce website, or online store, is a platform that allows customers to buy and sell products and services over the internet. E-commerce websites can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection, unlike physical stores that are limited to a specific location.

E-commerce websites can include: Product listings, Product categories, Customer reviews, A home page that represents the brand, Product pages that encourage shoppers to buy, A checkout process that's easy to use .
